What's In It For You?

Free View offers an extensive and carefully developed range of courses across a broad spectrum of subject areas. The curriculum spans creative industries, digital and technology, business and enterprise, health and social care, construction and engineering, and community learning, providing learners with access to both specialist and multidisciplinary study options. Each course is designed with a strong focus on practical, real-world application, combining industry-relevant knowledge with hands-on experience. Learners are supported in building technical ability, confidence, and transferable skills that prepare them for progression into employment, further training, or higher education.
