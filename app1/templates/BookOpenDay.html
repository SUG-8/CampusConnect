WHAT TO EXPECT FROM OUR OPEN DAY

during our open day you'll be exposed to:

  • Guided tours of the Free View campus, including classrooms, studios, labs, and social spaces
  • Live demonstrations and sample lessons from our teaching staff
  • Q&A sessions with current students about life at Free View
  • Presentations on courses, entry requirements, and progression routes
  • Information stands for student support, financial aid, and extracurricular activities
  • Opportunities to explore accommodation options and local amenities
  • Hands‑on workshops to experience the Free View learning environment
  • Refreshments and networking areas to chat with staff and students

WHO SHOULD ATTEND?

  • Students considering applying to Free View for the upcoming academic year
  • Parents, guardians, or carers wanting to learn more about the college
  • Mature learners exploring a return to education
  • Teachers or advisors supporting students with their next steps
  • Anyone curious about Free View’s courses, facilities, or community
